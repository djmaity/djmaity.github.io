Hi!

I am a computational biologist. I use molecular dynamics (MD) simulations to understand how protein molecules perform many intricate functions in living things. After having to analyze numerous MD simulations, I developed a python package, MD DaVis (https://djmaity.github.io/md_davis/), to automate the process. It can create interactive plots and visualizations, which alleviates the need to create multiple static plots. Check out my profile below to know more about me and my work.
