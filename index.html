About Me

I am a computational biologist. I use molecular dynamics (MD) simulations to understand how protein molecules perform many intricate functions in living things. After analyzing numerous MD simulations, I developed a Python package, MD DaVis (https://github.com/djmaity/md-davis), to automate the process. It can create interactive plots and visualizations, alleviating the need for multiple static plots. Check out my profile below to learn more about me and my work.
