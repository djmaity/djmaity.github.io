Hi! I am Dibyajyoti Maity

I am a doctoral student in the Department of Mathematics at Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru. I work in the field of computational biology and perform molecular dynamics simulations of protein molecules. I have built a python package for analysing dynamics of electric field around a protein molecule, called MD Davis. Please visit https://djmaity.github.io/md_davis/ to know more.
